Soil Filter cum drainage System for landscape

Drainage cell is a lightweight interlocking plastic structural module used for sub-surface drainage or to form tanks for underground water percolation and storm water management systems. Drainage cell modules are easily interlocked in the same plane or at right angles to one another.

These high compressive strength tanks are used as soak-away systems for rainwater from roofs and other surfaces such as parking areas, driveways, playgrounds and sports areas

Typical areas of applications include:

Landscape decks & planter boxes
Plaza decks, balconies & poolside
Sports fields & golf courses
Concealed drains
Retaining/basement walls
Storm drains
Capillary irrigation
Pond filtration
Percolation tanks
Landfills
